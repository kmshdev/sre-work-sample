# Incident Note

Answer this prompt in your own words:

> At 09:37, the service is reachable and latency is normal, but `bravo` has not
> received fresh ticks for 11 minutes. `alpha` and `charlie` are fresh.
> Operators ask whether downstream work should continue. What do you do in the
> first 30 minutes?

Cover:

- Severity and why.
- First five checks.
- What you block and what remains allowed.
- Who you notify.
- Operator-facing status.
- First runbook action.
- Evidence needed to recover, downgrade, or close the incident.
