Health is not just whether the process is running

A feed can be alive and still unsafe because the newest tick is stale, missing, malformed, or untrusted. Make that state visible and fail closed for unsafe actions.

Minimum operator view

FieldMeaning
aliveThe feed consumer can answer locally.
last_tick_age_seconds Age of the newest known tick.
freshness_status fresh, stale, or unknown.
safe_to_serve Whether unsafe downstream work may proceed.
allowed_actions Actions still permitted for this feed.
blocked_reason Specific reason work is blocked.

Expected states

  • Fresh: alive, recent tick, unsafe actions allowed.
  • Stale: alive, old tick, unsafe actions blocked.
  • Unknown: missing signal or unreachable feed, unsafe actions blocked.
  • Recovered: fresh signal restored and eligibility reopened.

Common pitfalls

  • A health endpoint that only reports HTTP 200 or process liveness.
  • Restart-only recovery without proving freshness returned.
  • Blocking every feed when only one feed is stale.
  • Alerts that do not tell an operator what to do next.
